Biryani

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish popular in South Asia, made with spices, rice, and meat.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken/Mutton
  • Yogurt
  • Biryani Masala

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat with yogurt and spices.
  2. Boil the rice until 70% cooked.
  3. Layer the meat and rice in a pot.
  4. Cook on low heat (Dum) for 20 minutes.
