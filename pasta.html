Creamy White Sauce Pasta

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Penne or Fusilli pasta
  • 2 tablespoons Unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons All-purpose flour
  • 1.5 cups Whole milk
  • 1/2 cup Shredded Parmesan or Mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Dried oregano or red chili flakes

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is boiling, melt the butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Whisk in the flour and cook for about 1 minute until the mixture is bubbly and light golden.
  4. Gradually pour in the milk while whisking constantly to prevent any lumps from forming.
  5. Continue to cook and stir until the sauce thickens. Stir in the cheese, salt, pepper, and herbs.
  6. Drain the pasta and toss it directly into the sauce. Serve warm with an extra sprinkle of cheese.
